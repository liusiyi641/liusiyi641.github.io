**25% of unambiguous, open domain questions can lead to conflicting contexts when retrieved using Google Search.**

![Google Search Results with conflicts](/images/google_results.png)

We study how open-domain question answering systems handle conflicting contexts retrieved from the web. Our findings reveal that a surprisingly large fraction of straightforward questions lead to conflicting evidence when using real search engines.
