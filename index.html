About

I'm third year PhD student at Department of Computer and Information Science, University of Pennsylvania, advised by Prof. Dan Roth

My primary research interest lies within the areas of natural language processing. Currently, I’m interested in studying various types of conflicts in the applications of Large Language Models (LLMs), including hallucinations (generations conflicting with contexts), knowledge conflicts (conflicts among contexts or between contextual and parametric knowledge), and also perspectives (conflicts among different sources of perspectives).
